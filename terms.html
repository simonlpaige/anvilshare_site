The audit

The audit costs $500, paid before work begins. We deliver a written report within the timeframe agreed at the start of the engagement (typically two business days after the initial call). You own the report. You may share it, use it to negotiate with other vendors, or discard it. The audit fee is non-refundable once work has started.

The build

Build engagements are scoped and quoted after the audit. A separate service agreement covers each build, specifying scope of work, timeline, milestone payments, and acceptance criteria. Code produced during the build is owned by you. We retain the right to reuse general-purpose patterns and libraries (not your business data or custom logic) in future engagements.

The savings split

If you choose the savings split model, we receive 50% of verified monthly savings for 36 months. "Verified savings" means the documented difference between your old software cost and your new operating cost (hosting, maintenance). Verification is based on your final invoice from the old vendor and the actual monthly cost of running the replacement.

You may end the savings split at any time. If you stop paying, we stop receiving our share. The software keeps running. There is no penalty, clawback, or termination fee.

Code ownership

You own the code we build for you. It runs on hardware you control. You can modify it, hire someone else to maintain it, or stop using it. We do not retain any proprietary claim on your custom code or your business data.

Data handling

We do not access, store, or process your customer data during audits. During build engagements, we may have temporary access to your server for deployment purposes. That access is revocable by you at any time. See the privacy policy for full details.
