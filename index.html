What's replaceable

Dispatch software

The scheduling and routing tools your office manager fights with every morning. Usually the most expensive line item and the easiest to replace.

Field service management

Job tracking, work orders, and the mobile app your techs use in the field. Most shops use 30% of the features and pay for 100%.

Invoicing

Billing, payment collection, and the QuickBooks integration that never quite worked. A replacement that actually syncs saves hours every week.

Scheduling

Appointment booking, calendar management, and customer notifications. Simple enough that a custom build costs a fraction of the subscription.

Point of sale

Counter and field payment processing. If your POS costs more than 2% of transactions plus a flat monthly fee, it's worth a look.

Inventory and basic CRM

Parts tracking, customer records, and service history. The kind of thing a spreadsheet almost handles but shouldn't have to.
