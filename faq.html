Questions
before the call.

Short answers about the cockpit, the audit, pricing, data, and what we will not touch yet.

  • Is this ChatGPT?

    No. It may use outside models when that is the right tool, but the useful part is the memory, schedule, dashboard, and checks around the model.

  • Where does my data live?

    The default is local-first. We keep business memory in files you control and avoid sending private context where it does not need to go.

  • What does a pilot cost?

    Founder pilot is $500 setup and $99 a month. Operator pilot is $1,500 setup and $199 a month.

  • Can it replace my SaaS?

    Sometimes. The cockpit usually comes first. After it watches the work for 30 days, we can see which paid tool is worth replacing.

  • What will you not do?

    We do not start with regulated medical work, legal advice, payroll, banking documents, or anything where a wrong answer can hurt people.

  • How fast can this start?

    A small pilot can usually start within a week. The first useful checks should be running in days, not quarters.

Good software should feel like a quiet shop tool: close at hand, easy to inspect, and boring enough to trust.
