Most small businesses keep their thinking in a dozen places. A CRM nobody updates. A Google Drive nobody can find. An inbox the owner can't escape. A notebook in someone's bag. When a question comes up like "who did we quote last month for that big job?" the answer takes an hour to dig up, or it never gets dug up at all.

Anvilshare builds you one brain that remembers everything for you, that you talk to like a person, and that quietly gets smarter the more you use it. That's the offer.

A private assistant for your business

  • It reads everything you've already got. Your customer list, past quotes, invoices, notes, the emails you choose to feed it, your website analytics. Nothing leaves your control. Nothing gets sold to anybody. It lives on infrastructure you own.
  • It answers questions in plain English. "Which customers haven't bought in 90 days?" "What's the average ticket on Tuesdays?" "Who did we send a quote to last month that never wrote back?" You ask. It tells you.
  • It spots money you're losing. It runs a monthly audit on its own and tells you what you're paying for that you don't use, what's underperforming, and where there's an obvious upsell sitting on the table.
  • It drafts the boring stuff. Follow-up emails, customer thank-yous, vendor cancellations, monthly reports. You approve, it sends. Or you handle the sending. Your call.
  • It gets sharper every week. Every conversation, every decision, every win and miss feeds back in. Month one it's helpful. Month three it knows your business as well as you do.

Things Single Brain is not

  • Not a chatbot for your website. Different product. Ask if you want one.
  • Not ChatGPT with extra steps. ChatGPT forgets you between sessions. This doesn't.
  • Not "AI that runs your business." It's a tool. You still run the business.
  • Not a $500/mo SaaS subscription that goes up every year. You own it. It runs on your hardware or a small server Anvilshare sets up for you. Costs are transparent and small.

Three layers, plain language

01

The memory

A markdown-based knowledge base that holds everything about your business in human-readable files. Not a database that locks you in. Open the folder and read it yourself.

02

The brain

A locally-running AI model, or a hosted one if you prefer, that reads the memory, answers questions, and writes drafts. You pick your privacy level.

03

The surfaces

A chat window on your phone, your desktop, or a Slack channel your whole team can talk to. Same brain, wherever you are.

The whole thing is built on open tools. If Anvilshare disappears tomorrow, you can hand the folder to anyone competent and they can keep it running. No vendor lock-in. No "we own your data." No subscription rugpull.
